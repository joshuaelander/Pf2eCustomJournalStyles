My Custom Journal Styles

A lightweight Foundry VTT module that adds CSS themes for Journals.

Installation

Copy the Manifest URL: https://github.com/YOUR_USERNAME/my-journal-styles/releases/latest/download/module.json

Open Foundry VTT > Add-on Modules > Install Module.

Paste the URL into the bottom field and click Install.

How to use

In any Journal Entry, switch the editor to HTML/Source Code Mode (usually the < > button). Wrap your content in one of the corresponding div tags below.

1. Classic Fantasy

Best for: Standard D&D or Pathfinder (Kingmaker/Rise of the Runelords).

<div class="theme-fantasy">
    <h1>Chapter 1: The Village</h1>
    <p>Text goes here...</p>
</div>


2. The Vaults (Dark Fantasy)

Best for: Abomination Vaults, Underdark campaigns, Dungeons.
Features: Dark background, light grey text, purple magical accents.

<div class="theme-vaults">
    <h1>Level 3: The Library</h1>
    <blockquote>"The shadows here are thick enough to choke on."</blockquote>
    <p>You enter a dark chamber...</p>
</div>


3. Magic Academy (Scholarly)

Best for: Strength of Thousands, Wizard Colleges, Elven Cities.
Features: Clean ivory background, gold accents, elegant headers.

<div class="theme-academy">
    <h1>Magical Theory 101</h1>
    <p>Please open your textbooks...</p>
</div>


4. Gunsmoke (Steampunk/Western)

Best for: Outlaws of Alkenstar, Eberron, Industrial settings.
Features: Grungy paper, brass borders, typewriter fonts.

<div class="theme-gunsmoke">
    <h1>Wanted: Dead or Alive</h1>
    <p>Reward: 5,000 gp</p>
</div>


5. Sci-Fi

Best for: Starfinder, Cyberpunk, Mothership.

<div class="theme-scifi">
    <h1>Log Entry 044</h1>
    <p>System Initialized...</p>
</div>


6. Horror

Best for: Call of Cthulhu, Gothic Horror (Blood Lords).

<div class="theme-horror">
    <h1>The Last Will</h1>
    <p>I leave everything to the cat...</p>
</div>
